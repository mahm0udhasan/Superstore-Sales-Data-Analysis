# Superstore Sales Data Analysis Project 📊

## 📌 Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing the performance of a retail **Superstore** dataset. The goal is to uncover key insights regarding sales trends, profitability, and regional performance to provide actionable recommendations for stakeholders.

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack Used
* **SQL:** For data cleaning, transformation, and querying key metrics.
* **Power BI:** For building an interactive dashboard and visualizing the final insights.
* **Excel:** Initial data exploration.

---

## 🔍 Key Insights & Executive Summary
Here are the major findings from the data:
* **Financials:** The business generated **$2.30M** in total sales with **$286.82K** in total profit (a **12.49%** profit margin).
* **Top Categories:** **Technology** is the leading category in sales and profit. **Furniture** has high sales but lower profit margins.
* **Geographics:** **California** and **New York** are the strongest markets in profit contribution.
